Mock Placement
Target Participants: Semesters 4, 6 of all branches of Engineering.

Description: Mock placement for the benefit of students.

The Number of Rounds: 
Round 1: Written Test (Technical & Aptitude)
Round 2: Group Discussion
Round 3: Personal Interview

Rules*: 
1	For a written test a cut off will be decided & on that basis, participants will be proceed further for round 2.
2	In G.D. the judging will be based on the communication skill, initiation ability, content.
3	For P.I. the judging will be done by the judges or the interviewer.
